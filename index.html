About the Human Purpose Framework

Human Purpose Framework (HPF) is a practical education and AI-alignment model that guides curriculum design, assessment, and lifelong human development. Institutions use HPF to create domain-based learning pathways, measure growth, and align AI tools with human-centered goals.

Vision

Build a civilization where human growth, ethical technology, and systemic development move forward through a shared purpose and measurable progress.

Mission

Help individuals, communities, educators, and systems structure human purpose into practical learning pathways, better decisions, and stronger outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for educators, researchers, and teams exploring human-centered education and AI alignment.

What is Human Purpose Framework?

Human Purpose Framework is a holistic education and AI alignment model built around seven domains: observing, understanding, thinking, deciding, performing, experiencing, and repeating.

How does HPF support curriculum design?

HPF helps educators create structured learning pathways, clearer outcomes, and more balanced skill development across cognition, action, reflection, and continuous improvement.

Can HPF be used for AI alignment?

Yes. HPF gives AI systems a human-centered structure for interpretation, decision support, and safer alignment with educational and developmental goals.
