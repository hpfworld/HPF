Observing Domain

(PCAREIA)

Observing is the foundation of awareness, where people gather sensory information, focus attention, and notice what matters most.

Seven Subdomains

Explore each subdomain with dedicated video content and detailed explanations

Perceptual Observing

P

Gathering sensory information through sight, sound, touch, taste, and smell to understand the physical world around us.

Video: Enhancing Perceptual Awareness

Continuous Observing

C

Maintaining attention to evolving changes over time, tracking patterns and developments in dynamic situations.

Video: Sustained Attention and Monitoring

Analytical Observing

A

Breaking down observations to detect patterns, relationships, and underlying structures in complex information.

Video: Pattern Recognition and Analysis

Reflective Observing

R

Looking back on experiences and observations to gain deeper insights and understand their significance.

Video: Reflective Practice and Insight

External Observing

E

Awareness of the outside world, including environmental factors, other people, and external circumstances.

Video: Environmental Awareness

Internal Observing

I

Awareness of self, including thoughts, feelings, bodily sensations, and internal mental processes.

Video: Self-Awareness and Mindfulness

Active Observing

A

Engaged and intentional attention, deliberately focusing awareness on specific aspects of experience.

Video: Focused Attention and Intentional Awareness
